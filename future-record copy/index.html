History is constant. We are living through it and yet simultaneously trying to preserve the past and future. The future of history exists digitally. It is immersive and well-preserved. This evolved from a panic of undocumented moments in the 2010s and 2020s. Society was so fearful of not capturing everything around them that the world that existed then exists now, in a unique form.
